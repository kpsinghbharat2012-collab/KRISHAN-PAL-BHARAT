# KRISHAN-PAL-BHARAT
BIOGRAPHY
Krishan Pal ‘Bharat’ was born on July 20, 1978, in the humble village of Ahera, located in Baghpat district, Uttar Pradesh, as the fifth child of Smt. Ludo Devi and Sri Garib Ram. Growing up amidst poverty and limited resources, he displayed an unwavering determination to rise above his circumstances. With a thirst for knowledge, he earned multiple master’s degrees in English, Hindi, Education and Philosophy from various esteemed universities.
Starting his career as a primary teacher with the MCD, Krishan Pal advanced through the ranks, becoming a TGT (English) and later serving as a Lecturer of English in Delhi Government schools. Beyond his teaching career, he ventured into the world of cinema, wearing many hats as a film scriptwriter, actor, and director. His creative ventures for YouTube channels have resulted in acclaimed films such as Fazeeta, Alajh-Palajh, Ghazab, Joda Thaat Ka, Bacche Kam, Mutthi Bhar Anaj, Laal Saadi, Laalu, Master Kaise Kaise, Geeta, Naari, Sujata, and many more.

•	Krishan Pal ‘Bharat’ is a multi-faceted personality whose contributions as an educator, storyteller, filmmaker, and writer continue to inspire and captivate audiences across generations. His journey from the modest beginnings of Ahera village to an acclaimed literary and artistic figure is a testament to the power of resilience, talent, and passion.
